The idea

I have a desire to develop a personal website to showcase my work and skills, which can help me in job interviews and advancing my career. But recently, I've discovered something more important in life.

Previously, I used to believe that in order to make a meaningful impact on the lives of the underprivileged, I had to be wealthy since many things in the world require money, not an action or idea can solve.

However, I have come to realize that by striving to become well-known and influential, I can actively contribute to improving the lives of those in need.


Through my own experiences, I have learned the power for positive mindset. It's all about having the right attitude and believing in yourself.

When you think positively, you can achieve great things. That's why I no longer believe in the idea that larger expectations lead to larger disappointments.

Instead, I'm eager to embark on my journey by sharing the concept of Elysium, and I hope it will inspire others and bring them a sense of joy.
